BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound-157)
EMERGING

Pentadecapeptide that promotes tissue repair, angiogenesis (blood vessel formation), and healing. Primarily studied in animal models with limited human trials.

Tissue repair Gut healing Angiogenesis Neuroprotection
📄 How to Find Research:
Search PubMed for:
BPC-157
BPC-157 wound healing
Key authors: Seiwerth, Gwyer
⚠️ FDA WARNING (2023): BPC-157 banned from US compounding pharmacies due to insufficient safety data.
